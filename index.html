Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order a custom cake?

→

You can order a custom cake by visiting our bakery or placing an order online at least 48 hours in advance. For wedding cakes and large event orders, we recommend ordering 2-4 weeks ahead. Please provide details about size, flavor, design, and any dietary requirements.

Do you deliver cakes?

→

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 25-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees start at $10 and may vary based on distance and order size. For wedding cakes or large orders, please contact us directly to arrange special delivery options.

Can I pick up my order?

→

You can place an order online and select the "Bakery Pickup" option at checkout. We'll notify you when your order is ready, typically within 24 hours. Please bring your order confirmation and ID when you come to pick up your sweet treats.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

→

Yes, we offer gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and vegan options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order so we can ensure your cake meets your specific needs.

How should I store my cake?

→

For best freshness, store your cake in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Cakes with cream cheese, whipped cream, or custard fillings should be refrigerated. Most cakes are best enjoyed within 3-5 days of purchase. For longer storage, you can freeze unfrosted cakes for up to 2 months.
